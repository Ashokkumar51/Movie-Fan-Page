Iron Man

Robert Downey Jr.'s portrayal of Iron Man (Tony Stark) is iconic, launching the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) to global fame. As a genius billionaire and inventor, Tony Stark dons the Iron Man suit to protect the world, blending cutting-edge technology with a sharp wit.
